‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is nachna free to use?
Yes, the nachna app is completely free to download and use. You only pay for the workshops you choose to book through our partner platforms.
How do I book a workshop?
Find a workshop you like and tap the "Register" button. You'll be redirected to the booking platform where you can complete your payment securely.
Can I cancel a workshop booking?
Cancellation policies vary by studio and workshop. Please check the specific terms when booking or contact the studio directly.
Why can't I see some workshops?
Workshops may have limited capacity or specific requirements. Some workshops might also be for certain skill levels or age groups.
How do I update my profile?
Go to the Profile tab, tap the edit icon, and update your information. Don't forget to save your changes!
Is my data secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard encryption to protect your data. Your password is securely hashed, and profile pictures are stored safely in our database.
